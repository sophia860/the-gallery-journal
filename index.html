Journal

Stories rooted in the landscape

Drop-caps, pull quotes, and airy paragraphs keep the prose legible and lingering—designed for slow reading across devices.

In the hush between cedar and cloud, we gather fragments of green—moss pressed beneath palm, water mirroring the sky. Each piece curated in the Journal is an echo of that stillness, a reminder that design can feel like fresh air.

“Design that breathes, emerging quietly from the understory.”

Our layouts stretch within a 1200px frame, giving typography the room it deserves. Display headings in Playfair Display anchor each section, while Inter carries the body with clarity and warmth. Labels remain uppercase, steady and calm.
